  7. You need to contact with the parking manager in case you have lost your vehicle reciept or other stuff.

Parking Rules

  1. You cannot abandon or park your vehicle or trailer at any public place in a position that causes obstruction or inconvenience, or poses danger to other people.
  2. If there are any road signs or markings indicating the manner of parking, your vehicle should be parked accordingly.
  3. On the wrong side of the road
  4. On roads with a white unbroken line in the centre
  5. In front of a gate or blocking the entrance to any premises
  6. Near a bus stop, school, hospital entrance
  7. Near any road sign if your vehicle obstructs the view of the sign
  8. Near a road crossing, a bend, at the top of a hill, or near and on a bridge.
  9. Near a traffic signal or a pedestrian crossing
